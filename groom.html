I Treat Your Dog Like Its a Best In Show Dog.

A Little About Our Groomer

My name is Barbara Tyson, and I have had a lot of experience with grooming dogs. I also groom cats aswell. I have been working on a lot of qualifications to get where I am today with opening my own dog grooming business, these are listed below.

Barbaras Qualifications Include

  • Certificate in Grooming and Canine Beauticianship Diploma.


  • A Distance Learning Dog Grooming Certificate.


  • NPC City and Guilds Certificate in Dog Grooming.


  • Pet First Aid Certificate.


  • One to One Training with Top Groomers who cut and show dogs.


I do a one to one grooming session with your dog so I can focus on that one dog and meet every customers needs and preferences.

I use a variety of top class products which are tailored to suit your specific dogs needs.

I do not keep any dogs in cages, because it is a one to one service with me and the dog, so there is no need to use a cage for locking any dogs up.
