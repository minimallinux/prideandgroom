About Us

Some information about Pride 'n' Groom.

A Little More About Us

At Pride 'n' Groom we have a lot of experience with grooming and pampering dogs (and cats), both small and large, from a Chichuaua to a Newfoundland.

What We Do At Pride 'n' Groom?

We do everything that a dog requires to look great, from having a bath, to being cut, having the dogs nails cut, anal glands and having ears and eyes cleaned. We also do handstripping for those breeds that require it.

The groomer Barbara has had a lot of practice with grooming dogs, and is qualified to City & Guilds standard.

The Salon has been fitted out to the highest standards for the well being of customers pets with the very best equipment and products being used.

We also sell arange of accessories and food.
